• GeoSn0w, another very friendly developer - before contacting him (same advice for Jake), take a look at his jailbreak oriented Youtube channel, F.C.E. 365 TV - iDevice Central
• Dennis Bednarz, UI designer of unc0ver app - contact him just if you noticed a serious UX bug
